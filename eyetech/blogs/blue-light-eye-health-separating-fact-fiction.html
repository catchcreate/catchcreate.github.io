Blue Light And Eye Health: Separating Fact From Fiction

  • Nabeel Amir
  • 11 Comments

In an age dominated by screens, concerns about the potential harm of blue light on our eyes have become increasingly prevalent. With many claims circulating about its effects, it's crucial to separate fact from fiction to understand how blue light truly impacts eye health.

While blue light from screens can contribute to digital eye strain and disrupt sleep patterns, it's important to approach the topic with a balanced perspective. While taking steps to reduce blue light exposure can be beneficial for eye comfort and overall well-being, there's no need to fearmonger or exaggerate its potential risks. By practicing good screen habits and incorporating strategies to minimize blue light exposure, we can enjoy the benefits of technology while safeguarding our eye health for the long term.
